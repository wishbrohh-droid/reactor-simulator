Reactor Simulator Guide

This simulator demonstrates the basic working of a nuclear reactor system.

Controls

Reactor Power – increases the energy produced by the reactor core.

Control Rods – inserting rods slows the nuclear reaction and reduces power.

SCRAM – emergency shutdown that inserts all control rods immediately.

Meters

Temperature shows the heat level inside the reactor core.

Pressure represents the steam pressure driving the turbine.

Radiation indicates radiation levels inside the reactor system.

This simulator is for educational visualization of reactor systems.
