Reactor Simulator Guide

This simulator demonstrates how a nuclear reactor system works.

Controls

Reactor Power increases the energy produced by the reactor.

Control Rods reduce the nuclear reaction when inserted.

SCRAM immediately shuts down the reactor.

Meters

Temperature shows reactor heat.

Pressure represents steam pressure driving turbines.

Radiation shows radiation level in the reactor.
